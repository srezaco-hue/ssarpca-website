About SSARP & Associates

SSARP & Associates, founded in 2012 by CA Mohammad Saem Reza, is a professionally managed CA firm based in Patna with a branch at Araria, serving clients across India and overseas.

We work as an integrated one-stop solution for:

  • Direct Tax, GST & International Tax advisory
  • Corporate law, ROC and secretarial compliance
  • Audit, due diligence and management reporting
  • Documentation, agreements and transaction support

Our philosophy is simple – professional excellence, integrity and client-centric service.

Firm History

A quick glimpse of our journey so far.

  • 2012 – Firm incorporated at Patna.
  • 2014–2018 – Expanded to GST, corporate law and NGO sector.
  • 2018 onwards – Handling complex scrutiny & appellate matters across India.
  • Today – Trusted advisor to domestic and international clients including Daewoo E&C, DFS GmbH and leading Indian entities.
