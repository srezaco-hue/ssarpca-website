Tax & GST Updates

A quick flavour of recent changes – talk to us for impact analysis on your business.

Income-tax – New Bill to replace 1961 Act

Finance Ministry – 2025 draft

The government has proposed a new Income-tax Bill to eventually replace the 1961 Act, with simplification of sections, more digital processes and faceless assessments. Businesses should expect higher reliance on documentation and e-proceedings in the coming years.

GST – Focus on invoice matching & GSTR-2B

GST portal updates

Authorities continue to emphasise invoice-wise reconciliation of ITC with GSTR-2B. Mismatches and ineligible ITC are a key area in departmental audits – ensure vendor compliances and monthly checks.

Compliance trend – Data-driven scrutiny

Across Direct & Indirect Tax

Returns, TDS/TCS, AIS/TIS data, e-way bills and bank information are being cross-linked. Clean bookkeeping, reconciliations and timely replies to notices are now more important than ever.
