Income Tax & GST ‚Äì Recent Highlights

Some key points frequently discussed with clients. (For exact provisions and latest notifications, please refer to official Government sources and Finance Acts.)

Income Tax

  • Faceless assessment and appeal processes now cover most scrutiny cases.
  • Higher TDS / TCS rates applicable in select cases where PAN or return filing is missing.
  • Multiple reporting requirements through AIS / TIS for high-value transactions.
  • New tax regime vs. old regime ‚Äì comparative planning required every FY for individuals / HUFs.

GST

  • Stricter ITC matching ‚Äì dependency on vendor filing behaviour (GSTR-1 & 3B).
  • Frequent changes in due dates, late fee and interest relief through notifications.
  • Department focussing on e-way bills, mismatches and ineligible credits in audits.
  • Need for proper documentation for export, refund and LUT related benefits.
